Applying a Gadget to a website

The Science Gadgets are openly available to be used as educational tools on any website. Educators are not only able to, but also encouraged to use the gadgets to enhance their site's potential learning experience. The code is open source, allowing programmers to adapt any part of a gadget they wish. The process of incorporating gadgets to a site is made very easy with minimalistic effort and expertise required.
