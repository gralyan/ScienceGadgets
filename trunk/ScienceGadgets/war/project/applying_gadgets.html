Applying a Gadget to a website

The Science Gadgets are openly available to be used as educational tools on any website. Educators are not only able to, but also encouraged to use the gadgets to enhance their site's potential learning experience. The code is open source, allowing programmers to adapt any part of a gadget they wish. The process of incorporating gadgets to a site is also made very easy with minimalistic effort and expertise required. A simple interface is available to create the HTML snippet desired. The following will demonstrate this process.

New Interactive Equation

Use the equation editor to create the desired equation. Start by selecting the expression you wish to change by touching or clicking on it. When an expression is selected, the toolbar at the bottom will be filled with all the relevant tools you may use to alter the expression. Once the desired equation has been created, you may click or touch the button labeled "Create Link" in order to show the hyperlink and iframe HTML snippet and their respective examples.
